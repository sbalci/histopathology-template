All results were presented as mean and standard deviation(SD) for continuous
variables and as number and percentage for categorical variables. Normality of Numerical
variables was tested by Kolmogorov-Smirnov test. Numerical data with normal distribution
was analyzed by Independent Samples t test while data with skewed distribution were
analyzed by Mann Whitney U test. Categorical variables were presented as percentages and
analyzed by Chi-square test.
The one-way analysis of variance (ANOVA) is used to determine any statistically
significant differences among more than two independent (unrelated) groups for normal
distribution.and Kruskal-Wallis is used to describe for abnormal distribution One-way
ANOVA and Kruskal-Wallis test is used to assess for significant differences on a continuous
dependent variable by a categorical independent variable for more than two groups,(Normal
and abnormal distribution, respectively). Tukey test was used to compare the differences
between the parametric variables. Dwass-Steel-Critchlow-Fligner test was used to compare
the differences between the non-parametric variables for differences between the groups.
Pearson Chi-Square test was used to compare the differences between categorical variables.
Pearson(normal distribution) and Spearman(abnormal distribution) correlation analysis was
used to evaluate the relationship between variables.

İSTATİSTİKSEL ANALİZ

Veriler IBM SPSS Statistics 23 programına aktarılarak tamamlanmıştır. Çalışma verileri değerlendirilirken kategorik değişkenler için frekans dağılımı (sayı, yüzde), sayısal değişkenler için tanımlayıcı istatistikler (ortalama, standart sapma) verilmiştir. 
Analiz sonucunda öncelikle varyans homojenliği için Levene testine, ardından farklılığın hangi grup ya da gruplardan kaynaklandığı “çoklu karşılaştırma testi”(Bonferonni ya da Tamhane’s T2) ile kontrol edilmiştir. Varyans homojenliğini sağlayan değişkenlerde gruplar arasındaki fark incelemesi için Bonferonni, varyans homojenliğini sağlamayan değişkenlerde gruplar arasında fark incelemesi için Tamhane’s T2 testine bakılmıştır. Ayrıca iki kategorik değişken arasındaki ilişkinin incelenemesinde ki kare testinden yararlanılmış ve sonuçlar tablolar halinde verilmiştir.


Statistical Analysis

Data were compiled with IBM SPSS Statistics 23 program. Categorical variables were defined with frequency distributions (number, percentage) and continious variables were defined with descriptive statistics (mean, standard deviation). One-way ANOVA was used to compare group differences. Levene test was used to evaluate whether the distribution is normal. Post-hoc tests were used to identify the different variables. Namely, Bonferonni and Tamhane’s T2 were used when variances are homogenous or not respectively. Distribution in categorical variables were evaluated using Chi-square tests.
